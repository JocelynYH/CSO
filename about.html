Cavalier Symphony Orchestra
First all-inclusive student-run full orchestra at UVA

No Auditions

Wait! To join CSO, I don't have to audition? Yup. You do not have to audition if you do not want to. Now, if you want to show off some of your skills or you're eyeing that section leader position, we'd love to hear you play.

Everyone Welcome

All skills levels and all instruments are welcome. Strings, brass, woodwinds, percussion, casual, experienced–we'd love to have you!

For Students, By Students

CSO puts you first. We are here to have a great time, make some new friends, and jam out to awesome music.

Our Tenants

Inclusivity

We're open to any orchestral musician with prior playing experience. We believe that if you love music, you should be able to continue playing in college. You don't have to strive to be a professional player or music major–just have fun and make music with CSO!

Fun

Friendship is key. We build strong bonds between our members and create a family within CSO. Excellence will be achieved if you have fun playing music!

Leadership in Music

We're completely student-run. Our members vote on what music to play, who conducts, and who sits on our executive board.

History

The Cavalier Symphony Orchestra was founded in 2015 by Jocelyn Huang. The idea began back in high school while she was searching, with no fruition, for a casual means to continue playing in college. Today, CSO provides a home to 103+ active musicians and fills the musical niche at the University of Virginia, while strengthening the arts community.

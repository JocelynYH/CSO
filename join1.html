Auditions

You are not required to audition to join CSO. The only exceptions to this rule are if you want to be a section leader, join the top group or play piano for us. We'd like to hear you play just to make sure you're as awesome as we already know you are.

Rehearsals

We have rehearsal or sectionals once a week, generally Sunday afternoons. For the non-audition group, rehearsals will only be 1 1/2 hours. The upper group will stay just one more hour after the non-audition group leaves. The time commitment is small for CSO because we value your time and understand that you all are busy people.

Repetoire

We play movie music (Jurassic Park, Star Wars, How to Train Your Dragon), musical music (Phantom of the Opera, Les Miserables), and full orchestra or big band music (New World Symphony, Carmen, Mars). How do we pick what we play? You suggest it. You vote it. We play it.

Instruments

All instruments are welcome. We will get you a part to play no matter what instrument. Members are expected to provide their own instruments, but rentals are available in the Charlottesville area. If you need any help whatsoever, please contact us.

To become a member

  • Email us through the contact page to let us know you are joining. This just helps us gauge how many people we are expecting for this semester.
  • Pay dues. You can either Venmo @CavalierSymphony-Orchestra or give cash to Michael Hilzendenger, mah3mu@virginia.edu.
  • Turn in forms. We'll need you to fill out and sign a photo release form and a liability form. If you are planning to rent a stand from us, please also fill out and sign a stand rental contract. Once you have completed all the necessary forms, please either scan them or give them to Virginia Cruser, vhc4cw@virginia.edu.

Once you're a member

  • Get music. We will hand out music folders at the first rehearsal. If you don't receive one or need different parts, please contact Melissa Zaleski, mlz5tb@virginia.edu.
  • Attend rehearsals. Please do attend every rehearsal you can to make the most of the time we have before our end-of-semester concert. If you know you have to miss a rehearsal, please email Virginia Cruser, vhc4cw@virginia.edu.

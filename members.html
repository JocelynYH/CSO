Leadership Opportunities

How can you get involved?


Section Leader

Make your section the best, damn section in the orchestra!


Section leaders lead sectionals and make sure their section can play the music.


Apply here. Applicants must be a member of CSO phil.

Conductor

You wave your hands and the rest of us play the music!


Conductors will generally conduct 1-2 pieces, rehearsing them with the orchestra.


Apply here. Applicants will conduct the orchestra for a few minutes during our conductor elections, after which the people will choose.

Executive Board

Make this orchestra's wheels turn! You directly shape CSO's future.


Positions include:

  • President - driving us forward
  • Vice President - internal management
  • Performance Director - everything rehearsals
  • Treasurer
  • Librarian - music printing
  • Social Chair - social event planning
  • Marketing/Recruitment Chair
  • Fundraising Chair
  • Historian - taking pictures
  • Chief Tech Officer - website and tech
  • Secretary - meeting minutes

Apply here. Applicants will give a short speech followed by a Q-and-A, after we will vote.

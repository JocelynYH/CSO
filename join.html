Joining FAQ

Can I join?

If you play an instrument, you can join. We welcome musicians of all levels — beginner through advanced. Remember, no auditions.

What instruments do you take?

All instruments welcome. We are looking especially for brass, winds, and percussion. Members are expected to provide their own instruments, but rentals are available in the Charlottesville area. If you need any help whatsoever, please contact CavSO@email.virginia.edu.

Do I have to audition to join?

No auditions

What type music do you play?

Members vote on our music. We'll play anything from Williams' Star Wars to Dvorak's New World Symphony to Holst's Mars.

How much does it cost to join?

Dues are $35 for one semester or $50 for an entire year. Renting a stand costs an additional $10 (our stands are heavy-duty, foldable stands, not wire stands). With these dues, you help us purchase repetoire, host social events, and provide music stands.

What's the time commitment?

Only 2 hours a week. Our rehearsals are generally Sundays, 1-3pm, in the Newcomb South Meeting Room. A more extensive calendar can be found on our members dashboard >>

What are some leadership opportunities?

  1. Section Leader

    A section leader is both a musical role and a social role. More information on how to become a section leader can be found here >>

  2. Exec Board

    The execs are the people behind the scenes making everything — social events, rehearsals, concerts — happen. More information on how to become an exec can be found here >>

  3. Conducting

    Conductors are student leaders that rehearse the band and lead the band during concerts. More information on how to become a conductor can be found here >>

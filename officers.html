Ben Barton
Performance Director
Question Answer
Instrument: Viola
Favorite Genre of Music: Depends on the moment: Classic rock, classical, jazz,blues,bluegrass ,etc.
Do you play any other instruments? Violin, guitars, electric bass, ukelele, Appalachian dulcimer and can pluck something out on most other string instruments.
Why did you pick the viola? Teachers from the University of Wyoming String Project came to my elementary school and demoed the various instruments. I loved the darker tone of the viola as soon as I heard it and also liked that not very many people play it.
List any quirks about yourself: I play viola. I feel that counts...
Fun Fact: I've played in 18 different orchestras/large string ensembles, 8 of which have been symphonies.

What do we stand for?

Inclusivity

We're open to any orchestral musician with prior playing experience. We believe that if you love music, you should be able to continue playing in college. You don't have to strive to be a professional player or music majorâ€“just have fun and make music with CSO!

Fun

Friendship is key. We build strong bonds between our members and create a family within CSO. Excellence will be achieved if you have fun playing music!

Leadership in Music

We're completely student-run. Our members vote on what music to play, who conducts, and who sits on our executive board.
